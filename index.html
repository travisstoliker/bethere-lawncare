About Us

We Show Up.
Every Time.

Payton Maas grew up on his family's eight-acre property, spending eight years learning what great lawn care actually looks like. In 2024, while still in high school, he turned that hands-on passion into a business. A Haslett native, athlete, and outdoorsman — Payton brings that same drive and work ethic to every property he maintains. The company grew quickly through the local community, with early roots tied to his father's Superior Cuts Barbershop, and a family legacy in the industry through a relative who once owned Langham Landscape and Design.

  • Founded by Payton Maas, lifelong Haslett resident
  • 8+ years of hands-on lawn care experience
  • Reliable, year-round service you can count on
  • Custom preferences honored — your yard, your way
  • Residential and commercial properties welcome
  • Snow plowing and removal when winter arrives
  • Same-day, free quotes — just call or text
